COVID-19

The Symptoms of COVID-19

The symptom’s strength can vary on every person from being mild or intense. Symptoms may include, but are not limited to:
  • Coughing
  • Sneezing
  • Trouble Breathing
  • Fever
  • Sore Throat
  • Headaches
  • Muscle Pain
  • Loss of Taste and Smell
Please don’t just use these as a diagnosis and instead go to a doctor right away if you have to. Some people show little to none symptoms so take precautions anyway.

Safety Precautions

During this time please quarantine yourself at home. If for any reason you have to leave the house please wear a mask and take a small bottle of hand sanitizer with at least 60 percent of 100% alcohol.[2][3] Please only use hand sanitizers when there are no other supplements such as soap and water.
Some people are trying to take advantage of the situation by making hand sanitizers that have 60% percent of alcohol that is not purely made of ethanol or ethyl alcohol. Some hand sanitizers can also include methanol and 1-propanol that can cause more health problems such as blindness, nerve damage, and death.[4] Please be careful before you buy. To take extra precautions you can also wear plastic gloves to prevent contact with the things other people would have touched.
